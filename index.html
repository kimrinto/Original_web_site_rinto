-at a night club-

One holiday eve, my two friends and I went to a nightclub in Cebu. It was our first experience in Cebu. We drank just a little and sometimes talked to girls. Then, we became friends with a girl we talked to. She said that she wanted to talk outside because it was noisy inside, so we went outside together. Soon after, her friends arrived there and we chatted for a while. They kept asking us to go out for a drink, so we decided to go along with them. We walked to the karaoke they recommended.

×

-in the karaoke room part1-

The karaoke was part of a large shopping mall, but it was dirty and had a suspicious atmosphere. We ordered snacks and drinks and started karaoke. The girls sang mostly and we enjoyed listening to them. It gradually got more and more exciting and one woman started dancing to the song. The other woman said she was drunk. The dances became more and more extreme, sometimes taking off their clothes. We enjoyed it without knowing anything about it.

×

-in the karaoke room part2-

The dances became more and more extreme and they even let us touch their bodies. We didn't refuse them because we didn't know that it was a trap. In a while, their smile suddenly disappeared and one woman said, "You guys must pay us. " In other words, they charged us money for the their dances. We were upset and refused the claim, but their attitude was strong. They made us give them all the money in our pockets, which they said was not enough, and demanded 10,000 pesos per person.

×

-the incident-

We resisted them from beginning to end, but they were accustomed and gave us no room to take advantage of them. They used their high heels and glass bottles as weapons and threatened to go to the dormitory to get the money. In addition, it turned out that the girl we talked to in the nightclub was a minor. They said that not the police were not on your side because this is their country. We put our lives first and finally accepted their request.

×

-in the end-

My friend and one woman went to the dormitory to get the money while me , my other friend, and the other women waited in the room. A short time later they returned and the payment was done. As a result, we were taken for all our cash, not just the 10,000 pesos we each were charged. My friend said they turned over every piece of luggage in the room. After that we were released. Overnight we lost a tremendous amount of money. We were at a loss, and it took a long time to recover.

The case happened in the eve of a holiday...
